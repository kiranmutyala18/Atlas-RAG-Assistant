# Acme Support Knowledge Base

Acme Cloud helps teams manage business workflows through dashboards, automations, reports, and team collaboration tools.

The platform is used by operations teams, customer support teams, and internal IT teams that need shared visibility into work.

Acme Cloud has three core plans:

- Starter for small teams getting organized
- Growth for scaling businesses that need automation and analytics
- Enterprise for advanced security, SSO, dedicated support, and custom compliance controls

The chatbot should explain product capabilities clearly and avoid inventing features that are not documented in the knowledge base.
